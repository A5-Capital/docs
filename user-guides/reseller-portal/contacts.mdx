## Overview

The **Contacts** sub-module is your primary ledger for managing individual customer relationships, tracking their transaction history, and managing their associations with organizations.

## Navigating the Contact Ledger

Access the main list of all customers to search, filter, and review high-level data.

### Steps to Access

1. Navigate to **Customers \> Contacts** via the sidebar.
2. Review the ledger columns for a quick overview:
   - **Customer**: The name of the individual.
   - **Email**: Primary contact email.
   - **Organization Name**: Linked company profile.
   - **Blocked Status**: Indicates if the contact is currently restricted.
   - **Joined Date**: When the contact was first added to the system.

### Customer Statistics

Click the arrow to expand the summary cards at the top of the ledger for an instant snapshot of your database health:

- **Total Customers:** The total number of unique contact records currently in your system.
- **Active Customers:** A count of all individuals with an unrestricted account status who can actively place orders and request quotes.
- **Blocked Customers:** A count of all accounts that have been manually restricted from platform interactions.

### Searching and Filtering

- **Search**: Use the input field **"Search by customer name, email, or phone number"** to find specific records.
- **Filter**: Apply the **"Block Status"** filter to refine results based on account standing.

## Customer Detail Page

Click on a specific **Contact Name** in the ledger to view their full profile and history.

The Contacts page serves as more than just a directory it is a functional command center. You can observe and interact with the  sales lifecycle from inquiry to  fulfillment without ever leaving the customer’s profile.

### Cards Summary

View the summary cards at the top of the page for an instant snapshot of:

- **Total Orders**: Count of all successful purchases.
- **Total Amount**: Cumulative monetary value of the customer.
- **Total Quotes**: Number of quotes requested.
- **Status**: Current account standing.

### Managing Profile Information

Within the **Customer Information** section, use the **Edit** button to keep your records current and manage account access. Clicking this button opens a management pane where you can update the following:

- **Primary Identity:** Modify the **First Name** and **Last Name** to ensure accurate personalization in communication and invoicing.
- **Communication Channels:** Update the **Phone Number** to maintain a direct line for order updates and support.
- **Access Control:** Utilize the **Block Status Toggle** to instantly restrict or restore a customer’s ability to interact with the platform.

<Note>
  Blocking a customer is a global action. Once toggled, the customer will be unable to place new orders or request quotes until they are manually unblocked.
</Note>

### Orders Information

Track purchase history with granular controls and detailed row data:

- **Toggle Views**: Switch between **Personal Orders** and **Organization Orders** tabs.
- **Search & Filter**: Find specific transactions using the **Order ID** search value and refine by **Order Status**.
- **Order Table Data**: Each entry provides visibility into:
  - **ID**: The unique order reference number.
  - **Created By**: The account responsible for the order.
  - **Subtotal**: The financial value of the order.
  - **Status/Type**: Current state (e.g., Pending) and payment method (e.g., Check).
- **Order Actions**: Click the three-dot menu icon `⋮` on any order row to:
  - **View Order**: See full transaction details.
  - **Cancel Order**: Stop the order process.
  - **Archive Order**: Move the record to historical storage.

### Quotes Information

Manage price inquiries and estimates with extended lifecycle actions:

- **Toggle Views**: Switch between **Personal Quotes** and **Organization Quotes** tabs.
- **Search & Filter**: Find specific quotes using the **Quote ID** search value and refine by **Quote Status**.
- **Quote Table Data**: Detailed visibility into:
  - **ID & Title**: The reference number and descriptive name of the quote.
  - **Quoted by**: The user account that generated the quote.
  - **Amount & Payment**: The total value and preferred payment method (e.g., ACH, Hubspot, Check).
  - **Status**: Current standing (e.g., Expired, Completed).
  - **Dates**: Tracks both the **Created at** and **Expired at** timestamps.
- **Quote Actions**: Click the three-dot menu icon `⋮` to access:
  - **View/Edit Quote**: Review or modify quote details.
  - **Generate Quote Link**: Create a shareable URL for the customer.
  - **Duplicate Quote**: Create a new quote based on existing data.
  - **Archive Quote**: Remove the quote from the active list.
