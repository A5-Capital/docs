## Overview

The **Companies** sub-module allows you to manage B2B entities (Organizations). From this module, you can control organizational memberships, set custom shipping and credit rules, and manage tax exemptions or price markups specific to a business entity.

## Navigating the company ledger

Access the list of all business entities to monitor their high-level performance and status.

### Steps to access

1. Navigate to `Customers > Companies` via the sidebar.
2. Review organization statistics directly in the ledger:
   - **Total Paid Orders**
   - **Total Unpaid Orders**
   - **Total Paid Amount**
   - **Total Purchased Items**

### Searching and filtering

- Use the **Search by organization ID, name** input field to locate a specific business.
- Apply the **Block Status** filter to refine your view (e.g., viewing only **Not Blocked** entities).

## Organization detail page

Click a specific **Organization Name** or **ID** to access the multi-tabbed management interface.

### Overview and member management

- **Update business details**: Click the **Edit** button in the **Overview** tab to modify the **Organization Name**, **Domain**, or **Business Number**.
- **Manage members**: Within the **Members** tab, you can search for members by **Name**, **Email**, or **Phone Number** and filter them by **Block Status**.
- **Invite users**: Navigate to the **Invitations** tab and click the red **\+ Invite Member** button. You can monitor invitation metadata such as **Email**, **Role**, **Status**, and **Sent At**.

### Orders and shipping rules

- **Transaction history**: In the **Orders** tab, search for specific transactions via the **Order ID** input or apply the **Order Status** filter (e.g., **Delivered**, **Pending**, **Confirmed**).
- **Shipping logic**: Navigate to the **Shipping** tab to:
  - Toggle the **Free Shipping** switch to **Enabled**.
  - Define a **Minimum Order Value**.
  - Click **Update Rules** to save your changes.

### Financial and tax configurations

- **Credit configuration**: In the **Credit Configuration** tab, you can toggle the **Unlimited Credit Limit** and **Allow Past AR Credit** switches. Click **Apply Credit Configuration** to save, or **Reset to Default** to restore global settings.
- **Tax exemptions**: Use the **Tax Exempt** tab to review jurisdictions across the **United States**, **Canada**, and **Mexico**. Click **Add Tax Exempt Request** to start a new filing.
- **Tax history**: Review historical filings in the **Tax Exempt Requests** tab. You can filter by **Status** or click the **Red Trash Icon** to delete a specific entry.

### Price adjustments

Navigate to the **Price Adjustments** tab to manage the **Storefront Markup Configuration**:

- Click **Update Markup** to modify the global **Standard Markup** percentage.
- Click **\+ Add Price Adjustment** to configure markups for specific products or brands.

### Contact cards

In the **Contacts** tab, manage the organization's point-of-contact grid:

- Click **\+ Add Contact** to create a new record.
- Click the **Red Pencil Icon** on a contact card to update the **Name**, **Role Badge**, or **Phone Number**.
