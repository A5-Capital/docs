The **Companies (Organizations)** module is the central business entity layer of the Reseller Portal. It provides the legal and commercial context required for downstream workflows in Quotes, Orders, Finance, and Approvals.

---

## 1. Discovery and statistics

The **Companies Ledger** is your entry point for monitoring business entities. Access it by navigating to **Customers \> Companies** in the sidebar.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search and filter">
    Use the **Search by organization ID, name** input field to find matching organization records. Filter the list by **Block Status** to view **Not Blocked** or active entities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review summary data">
    The top summary cards and table rows display stable, organization-level data:

    - **Total Paid Orders** and **Total Unpaid Orders**.
    - **Total Paid Amount** (Cumulative revenue).
    - **Total Purchased Items** (Volume).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 2. Profile and identity

Manage the core identity of the business and the primary personnel associated with the organization.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Overview">
    This tab houses the latest saved organization information and access status.

    1. **Review Stats**: View the organization-specific summary cards for orders and amounts.
    2. **Edit Profile**: Click **Edit** to modify the **Organization Name**, **Domain**, or **Business Number**.
    3. **Validate**: The system validates required fields before saving to ensure data integrity for downstream modules.
    4. **Audit**: Monitor **Access Status** (e.g., **Not Blocked**) and timestamps for **Created On** and **Last Updated**.

    <Warning>
      Updates to identity data propagate to all downstream modules. Incorrect mapping here can distort invoice ownership, tax treatment, and approval confidence.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Members">
    View platform users linked to this organization.

    - **Search & Filter**: Find members by name, email, or phone, and filter by **Block Status**.
    - **Table Data**: Review row-level values including **Customer**, **Role** (e.g., Owner), **Organization Name**, **Total Orders**, **Blocked** status, and **Joined** date.
    - **Readiness**: Ensure member readiness values (e.g., **Active**) are correctly rendered.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Invitations">
    Manage the lifecycle of new organizational user invites.

    1. **Invite**: Click the red **\+ Invite Member** button to open the modal.
    2. **Submit**: Enter user details and assign a **Role**. Once saved, the invite appears in the ledger.
    3. **Monitor**: Review the **Email**, **Role**, **Status**, **Expires At**, and **Sent At** columns.
    4. **Resend**: Click the **↺ Resend** button to re-trigger the invitation email.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Contacts">
    Manage primary points of contact for manual business coordination.

    - **Add**: Click the red **\+ Add Contact** button to create a new organizational contact.
    - **Edit**: Click the **Pencil Icon** on a contact card to update the name, role badge, or phone number.
    - **Delete**: Click the red **Trash Icon** to remove a contact member.
    - **Metadata**: Each card displays the **Name**, **Role Badge**, and **Phone Number**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 3. Transactional history

The **Orders** tab within the organization detail provides a centralized view of all business-linked transactions.

### Order management

- **Search & Filter**: Search by **Order ID** or filter by **Order Status** (e.g., Delivered, Pending, Confirmed, Partial).
- **Table Columns**: Visibility into **ID, Customer Name, Created By, Tags, Subtotal, Status, Order Type (Customer/Quote), and Payment (Card/ACH)**.
- **Row Actions**: Use the three-dot menu `⋮` to:
  - **View Order**: Access full transaction details.
  - **Cancel Order**: Stop a pending order process.
  - **Archive Order**: Move the record to historical storage.

## 4. Commerce and financials

Configure the transactional rules, credit limits, and pricing markups that govern organization behavior.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Credit Configuration">
    Manage financial risk and purchasing power.

    - **Modes**: Review if the configuration in **Settings** is in **Custom** or **Global** state.
    - **Toggles**: Enable or disable **Unlimited Credit Limit** and **Allow Past AR Credit**.
    - **Persistence**: Click the red **Apply Credit Configuration** button to save.
    - **Reset**: Click **Reset to Default** to restore global settings for the organization.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Price Adjustments">
    Control margins through storefront markups.

    - **Global Markup**: Click **Update Markup** to modify the global **Standard Markup %** for the organization.
    - **Targeted Rules**: Click the red **\+ Add Price Adjustment** button to configure specific product- or brand-level rules.
    - **Table Data**: Displays **Adjustment Type, Target, MPN / Brand Identifier, Markup %, Spiked Markup %, and Spike Expiry**.
    - **Spiked markup:** Special type of **Markup** that can be set for a limited time for a particular organization.
    - **Actions**: Use row-level controls to edit or **Delete** specific adjustment entries.

    <Info>
      The markup and pricing rules established for an organization are applied consistently across all touchpoints, ensuring that both quotes and the storefront reflect the same unified pricing structure for that client.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 5. Shipping and compliance

Handle jurisdictional logistics and tax-exempt status to ensure accurate checkout and reporting.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Shipping">
    Define organization-wide shipping rules.

    1. **Discovery**: Search for requests by **Organization ID/Name** and narrow your results by **Status**.
    2. **Review Data**: View essential details, including **Organization, Status, Request Message, and Request Date**, directly in the table.
    3. **Manage Requests**: Take immediate action on any request:
       - **Approve**: Verify authenticity and approve the tax exemption.
       - **Reject**: Decline the tax exemption request if it doesn’t meet requirements.
       - **Request Documents**: Ask the user for additional documentation to support their claim.
    4. **Cleanup**: Remove specific entries by clicking the red **Trash Icon**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Tax Exempt Requests">
    Audit the history of all tax exemption attempts.

    - **Discovery**: Search requests by **Organization ID/Name** and filter by **Status**.
    - **Table Data**: Review **Organization, Status, Request Message, and Request Date**.
    - **Interaction**: Requests can be Interaced With :
      - Approve: Users can Approve Tax Exemption Request after verifying its authenticity.
      - Reject: Users can reject tax exemption request.
      - Request for more Documents: Users can Request for More Documents.
    - **Cleanup**: Click the red **Trash Icon** to delete a specific request entry.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Tax Exempt">
    Review tax status across the US, Canada, and Mexico.

    - **Exemption Cards**: View cards showing exempt vs. taxable summary counts for each region.
    - **State Lists**: Review the taxable/exempt status of specific provinces or states.
    - **Initiate Tax Exemption**: Click the red **Add Tax Exempt Request** button to start a new tax exemption flow for that organization.
      - **Create Tax Exemption**: Fill the Form with Required Tax Exemption Documents and details in Additional Message section.

    <Info>
      Approved tax-exempt state supports downstream quote/invoice tax treatment of **\$0.00** where applicable.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
