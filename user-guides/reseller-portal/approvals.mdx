## Overview

The **Approvals** sub-module allows you to manage organization-level requests within the A5SYNC platform. You can review pending registrations, verify business documentation, and either approve or reject requests to manage your customer base effectively.

<Info>
  Organization requests are categorized into **Create** (new registrations) or **Update** (changes to existing profiles) types.
</Info>

## Managing the Request Ledger

Access the central repository of all organization requests to filter and find specific submissions.

1. Navigate to **Customers** > **Approvals** via the sidebar.
2. Use the **Search by organization ID, name** input field to locate a specific record.
3. Use the **Status** filter to view **Pending**, **Approved**, or **Rejected** requests.
4. Use the **Type** filter to distinguish between **Create** and **Update** requests.

### Ledger Columns
The ledger provides a high-level overview of the following data:
* **Organization**: The name of the business entity.
* **Domain**: The associated web domain.
* **Status**: Current state of the request.
* **Type**: The nature of the request (Create vs. Update).
* **Joined Date**: The timestamp of the initial submission.

## Creating an Organization Manually

If you need to bypass the standard user-facing registration flow, you can initiate a request manually.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Creation Modal">
    Click the red **+ Create Organization** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select or Create Customer">
    Search for an existing customer by name, email, or company. If the contact does not exist, click the **+ Create new customer** link to initiate a new record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Organization Details">
    Enter the **Organization Name** and the optional **Domain**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Documentation">
    Use the **Click to upload or drag and drop** area to attach necessary business verification files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click the **Create Organization** button to finalize the submission.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reviewing and Processing Requests

To take action on a request, you must enter the **Request Detail** page.

### Reviewing Details
1. Click a specific **Organization Name** or the **View Detail** link.
2. Review the **Organization Details** section for the Business Number and Domain.
3. Review the **Requested By** section to verify customer contact details and their active status.
4. Click the **Preview** button on uploaded **Verification Documents** to view PDFs or images directly.

### Taking Action
Use the following actions to process the request:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Approve">
    Click the green **Approve Request** button. This promotes the request to an active **Organization** status within the system.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Reject/Request Info">
    * Click the red **Request More Documents** button to notify the requester of missing information.
    * Click the red **Reject Request** button to decline the registration entirely.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Delete">
    To remove a record permanently from the ledger, click the red **Delete** (trash) icon in the request list.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  Check the **Activity Log** at the bottom of the detail page to see the full chronological history of any comments or status changes for the request.
</Tip>
