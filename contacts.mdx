## Overview

The **Contacts** sub-module is your primary ledger for managing individual customer relationships, tracking their transaction history, and managing their associations with organizations.

## Navigating the Contact Ledger

Access the main list of all customers to search, filter, and review high-level data.

### Steps to Access
1.  Navigate to **Customers > Contacts** via the sidebar.
2.  Review the ledger columns for a quick overview:
    * **Customer**: The name of the individual.
    * **Email**: Primary contact email.
    * **Organization Name**: Linked company profile.
    * **Blocked Status**: Indicates if the contact is currently restricted.
    * **Joined Date**: When the contact was first added to the system.

### Searching and Filtering
* **Search**: Use the input field **"Search by customer name, email, or phone number"** to find specific records.
* **Filter**: Apply the **"Block Status"** filter to refine results based on account standing.

---

## Customer Detail Page

Click on a specific **Contact Name** in the ledger to view their full profile and history.

### Executive Summary
View the summary cards at the top of the page for an instant snapshot of:
* **Total Orders**: Count of all successful purchases.
* **Total Amount**: Cumulative monetary value of the customer.
* **Total Quotes**: Number of quotes requested.
* **Status**: Current account standing.

### Managing Profile Information
In the **Customer Information** section, click the **Edit** button to modify the individual's profile details.

### Orders Information
Track purchase history with granular controls:
* **Toggle Views**: Switch between **Personal Orders** and **Organization Orders** tabs.
* **Search**: Find specific transactions using the **Order ID** search.
* **Filter**: Refine the list using the **Order Status** filter.

### Quotes Information
Manage price inquiries and estimates:
* **Toggle Views**: Switch between **Personal Quotes** and **Organization Quotes** tabs.
* **Search**: Find specific quotes using the **Quote ID** search.
* **Filter**: Refine the list using the **Quote Status** filter.

---

## Organization Contacts

Manage members specifically associated with a business organization.

### Viewing Members
1.  Navigate to an **Organization Detail** page.
2.  Select the **Contacts** tab to view the list of associated members.

### Managing Members
Each member is displayed as a contact card showing their **Name**, **Phone Number**, and **Role Badge** (e.g., *Accounts*, *Others*).

* **Add Member**: Click the **+ Add Contact** button within the organization contacts tab to create a new member record.
* **Edit Member**: Click the **red "Edit" (pencil) icon** on a contact card to update their info.
* **Delete Member**: Click the **red "Delete" (trash) icon** on a contact card to remove them from the organization.

<Tip>
  Using the Role Badge is an efficient way to identify the primary points of contact for billing or technical inquiries within an organization.
</Tip>
